Serradura

Description

This is a quick and easy dessert that is guaranteed to delight everyone!

Ingredients

  • One package teatime cookies/biscuits
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • one can sweetened condensed milk
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Toasted sliced almonds
  • Toasted coconut
  • Passion fruit pulp and seeds

Steps

  1. Crumble cookies into fine crumbs (but not too fine!)
  2. In a mixer, beat cream by hand whisk or electrical until medium-soft peaks form
  3. Add sweetened condensed milk and continue whipping until medium-stiff peaks form, 2 to 4 minutes
  4. Spoon the cream into a resealable bag and use to pipe into a glass, refrigerate for at least four hours
  5. Cookies will soften, serve cold and garnish with cinnamon, almonds, coconut and passion fruit just before serving
