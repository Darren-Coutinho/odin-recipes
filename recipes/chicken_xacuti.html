Chicken Xacuti

Description

This is an ancient dish that combines tender, flavourful chicken with roasted coconut and spices. It is made for feasts, weddings and other events.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (whole, cut into curry pieces)
  • 1 tablespoon cumin powder
  • 1 tablespoon Olive oil
  • 6 whole cloves
  • 4 star anise
  • 2 tablespoons poppy seeds
  • 1 tablespoon black peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon fennel seeds
  • 1 cup grated coconut (must be fresh)
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon ground nutmeg

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat, roast coconut in the oil about 5 minutes, till it browns. Set aside.
  2. Next heat the spices, in the same skillet. Roast chilli, cinnamon, cloves, star anise, poppy seeds, cumin seeds, peppercorns, fennel, corriander, and turmeric until fragrant.
  3. Transfer to a grinder and grind with cooconut and garlic till a paste is formed
  4. Next, heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan over medium high heat. Satue onoions untill browned about 5 minutes. Add paste and cook untill oil separates. Add chicken and satue until brown and no longer pink in the centers, 7 to 10 minutes.
